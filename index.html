How MedTrack Works

We have delightful user experience

Register

Start your journey by creating an account or continue as a guest - no stress, no barriers. Your health tracking starts instantly.

Set Up Your Medication

Add your medication, dosage, and how often you take them. You can also input how many pills you currently have for refill tracking.

Customize Your Reminder

Choose when you want to be notified - morning, afternoon, night, or custom times. The reminders are genuine and consistent.

Stay on Track With Smart Alerts

Receive timely reminders and tag each dose as taken. Remind me later, or skip. Missed doses trigger smart alerts to keep you consistent.

Track Progress And Refill easily

View Your medication history, streaks, and receive alerts when your stocks runs low. MedTrack keeps you organized and in control.
